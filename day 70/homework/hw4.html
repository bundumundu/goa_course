All About Seals

Seals are amazing marine mammals. Learn more about them below!

Introduction to Seals

Seals are mammals that live both on land and in the ocean. They belong to a group called Pinnipedia, which also includes sea lions and walruses. Seals are great swimmers and can dive to deep ocean levels.

Types of Seals

There are two main types of seals:

  • True Seals: These seals do not have external ears and move by wriggling on their bellies when on land.
  • Eared Seals: These seals have visible ear flaps and can walk on land using their flippers.

Interesting Facts About Seals

  • Seals can hold their breath underwater for a long time, sometimes up to two hours.
  • The Weddell seal can dive to more than 600 meters deep.
  • Seals use their whiskers to detect prey in dark or murky waters.
  • There are about 33 species of seals worldwide.
