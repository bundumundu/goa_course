Hippos and Sea Lions

Hippos

Hippopotamuses, commonly known as hippos, are large, mostly herbivorous mammals native to sub-Saharan Africa. They spend much of their time in water to keep their bodies cool under the hot African sun.

Interesting Fact: Hippos can hold their breath underwater for up to five minutes!

Sea Lions

Sea lions are pinnipeds found in the coastal waters of the Northern Hemisphere and the southern hemisphere. They are known for their playful nature and intelligence.

Interesting Fact: Sea lions can dive to depths of over 600 feet in search of food!
